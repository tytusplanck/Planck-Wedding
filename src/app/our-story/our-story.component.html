Our Story

Tytus and Kelsey met in their high school days in the Lakota West Marching Band. As they applied to colleges their senior year, they planned to continue their relationship despite which colleges they both decided to attend. chose. Kelsey still believes to this day that they happened to choose the same school but in reality Tytus couldn’t stand the separation. In the fall of 2014, they started school at The Ohio State University. As they began this new journey in Columbus, their relationship only grew and their faith became an even larger part of their love for one another. On May 20th, 2017, the start of the summer before their senior year, Tytus popped the question and Kelsey said yes! They’re finishing up their senior year and on May 25th, 2018, they’ll tie the knot once and for all!
