Our Story

Tytus and Kelsey met in their high school days in the Lakota West Marching Band. Drumline + Colorguard turned out to be a perfect match, as the stereotype goes. As they applied to colleges their senior year, they planned to continue their relationship despite which colleges they both decided to attend [Kelsey still believes to this day that they just happened to choose the same university, but in reality, Tytus knew she was the one]. In the fall of 2014, they started school at The Ohio State University. As they began this new journey in Columbus, not only did their love for espresso grow, but also their love for one another. They got connected with a local church + watched their relationship thrive with their faith at the center of it all. On May 20th, 2017, the summer before their senior year, Tytus popped the question and Kelsey said yes! They’re finishing up their last semester of school and on May 25th, 2018, they’ll tie the knot once and for all!
